Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 13, 2026

Summary: QuickReact does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your emoji preferences are saved locally on your device only.

1. Information We Collect

QuickReact does not collect any personal information.

The extension stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Recently Used Emojis: Up to 50 of your most recently used emojis for quick access
  • Favorite Emojis: Emojis you mark as favorites for personalized quick access

This data never leaves your device, is stored using Chrome's local storage API, cannot identify you personally, and can be cleared at any time through Chrome's extension settings.

2. How We Use Information

The locally stored emoji preferences are used solely to display your recently used emojis and favorites for faster access. This data is never transmitted to any server or third party.

3. Third-Party Services

Giphy API: When you search for GIFs, QuickReact makes API requests to Giphy through our secure backend proxy. Only your search query is sent (e.g., "happy cat"). No personal identifiers are transmitted. No tracking cookies or tokens are used. Results are displayed directly and not stored.

Giphy's privacy policy: https://support.giphy.com/hc/en-us/articles/360032872931

4. Data Security

Since QuickReact stores data only locally on your device, your data is protected by your device's security. No data transmission means no interception risk. No server-side storage means no data breach risk.

5. Permissions Explained

storage: Required to save your emoji preferences locally on your device

activeTab: Required to insert emojis and GIFs into text fields when you click them

Host permissions: Required to fetch GIF search results from Giphy API

6. No Tracking or Analytics

QuickReact does not use Google Analytics, tracking pixels, cookies, usage telemetry, advertising networks, or third-party data collection.

7. Children's Privacy

QuickReact does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The extension is safe for all ages.

8. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data. Delete your data by clearing your favorites and recent emojis through Chrome's extension settings or by uninstalling the extension. No account is required. Your data is already on your device.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page and notify users through the Chrome Web Store update notes.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can open an issue on our GitHub repository, leave a review on the Chrome Web Store, or contact us through the support email listed on the Chrome Web Store.

11. Open Source

QuickReact's code is open source and can be audited by anyone. You can verify our privacy claims by reviewing the source code.

Bottom Line: QuickReact is designed with privacy as a core principle. We don't collect data, we don't track you, and we don't share anything because there's nothing to share.
